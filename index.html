A Great Chaos is the third studio album by American rapper Ken Carson. It was released through Opium and Interscope Records on October 13, 2023. The album is a follow-up to Carson's second studio album, X (2022); it consists of 18 tracks and features guest appearances from Destroy Lonely and Lil Uzi Vert.

A Great Chaos received generally positive reviews, with critics praising its appeal and production. The album was supported by the release of the single: "i need u", which was released on Valentine's Day. The album debuted at number 11 on the US Billboard 200 which was Ken Carson's first ever top 20 US charting album
